📍 Ranni's Rise, Liurnia

Quest Summary

Aid the mysterious lunar witch Ranni on her quest to claim the power of the Rune of Death. Follow her across multiple regions, defeat Starscourge Radahn to unlock a meteor event, explore Nokron and Ainsel River, and ultimately witness the Age of Stars ending.

AI Tip

Don't miss the Fingerslayer Blade in Nokron — it's essential to progress Ranni's quest. Exhaust all her dialogue at each step.

Reward

🏆 Dark Moon Greatsword, Age of Stars ending
